## 🎁 A closer look at the four bonus rounds

- **Coin Flip** — the simplest and most frequent bonus: a red and a blue coin are each assigned a multiplier, the coin is tossed, and the side that lands up pays its value.
- **Cash Hunt** — a shooting-gallery grid conceals 108 multipliers behind symbols; you lock onto one before time expires, every tile is revealed together, and you collect what sat behind your pick.
- **Pachinko** — a puck bounces down a peg wall into a multiplier slot; a DOUBLE result doubles all the values and drops the puck once more.
- **Crazy Time** — the centrepiece, staged separately on a 64-segment virtual wheel with blue, green and yellow flappers. You choose a flapper, the wheel spins, and DOUBLE or TRIPLE segments re-spin with stacked multipliers right up to the 20,000x maximum.
